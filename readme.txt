Awesome Floating Form is a WordPress plugin that lets you add a floating contact button to your website. Visitors can click the button to send a contact request directly.

== Description ==
Awesome Floating Form allows users to add a contact button on top of their website. The button will be floating, so it will always be visible to visitors, no matter where they are on the page. Visitors can click on the button to send a contact request directly.

Awesome Floating Form is a great way to make it easy for visitors to contact you. It is also a great way to increase your website\'s conversion rate.

Here are some of the features of Awesome Floating Form:

Easy to use: Awesome Floating Form is very easy to use. Just install the plugin, add the contact button to your website, and you\'re good to go.

Floating button: The contact button will always be visible to visitors, no matter where they are on the page. This makes it easy for visitors to contact you.
Contact form: Awesome Floating Form comes with a built-in contact form. This makes it easy for visitors to send you a message.

Customization: Awesome Floating Form is highly customizable. You can change the look and feel of the contact button and the contact form to match your website\'s design.

If you are looking for a way to make it easy for visitors to contact you, then Awesome Floating Form is a great option. It is easy to use, highly customizable, and comes with a built-in contact form.
